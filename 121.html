121. CINTU CAKUTUSUNGUSHA

"It is a thing most wonderful"

1.  Cintu cakutusungusha,
Ica kupapusha nganshi,
Mwana Lesa aishile
Ukwisa fwilo mwaice.

2. Ninjishibe fyo ca cine;
Asalilyo kuicefya,
Aculishe, alifwile
Pantu mwine antemwisha.

3. Njishibe fyo engantemwa
'Ne mwaice mukakashi;
'Kutemwa kwakwe kwakula
Imfwi lyo kuti mutemwe.

4.  Ca  kupap pakwishiba
'Kutemwa kwakwe kwa cine
Lelo kupapo kucila
Apo mutemwa panini.

5. Kamutemwishe, mwe mfumu
Kamwimbile mu mutima,
Nakulaya ndamutemwa,
Nsuke mumone 'fyo mwaba.

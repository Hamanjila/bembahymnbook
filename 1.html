1.  MWE BANTU BONSE BA PANSHI

"All people that on earth do dwell"

1. Mwe bantu bonse ba panshi.
Mulumbanyeni mutote,
No kumutemwa iseni,
Imbeni pa cinso cakwe

2. Ibukisheni ni Lesa
Atubumbile fwe bonse,
Aletukumba nge mpanga,
Pantu ifwe tuli bakwe.

3. Ingileni no mucinshi,
Mupalameni no kwanga
Shina lyakwe lumbuleni,
Ici cine e cisuma.

4. Lesa wesu, e musuma,
Ali no luse kuli ifwe;
'Cine cakwe ciminina
'Nshiku ne nshiku shonse pe

29. BAKAKUMBA BAPEMPWILE

"While Shepherds watched their flocks by night"

 1. Bakakumba bapempwile
Mpanga ubushiku;
Umungeli aikile,
'Lulumbi lwa mulu.

2. "Mwilatina," asoseshe
Abali no mwenso,
"Namuletels fye bonse
"Ilandwe lya nseko."

3. "Mu mushi wa kwa Dabidi,
"Afyalilwe lelo,
"Mfumu Kristu, Mupusushi:
"E bulangililo;"

4. "Mwana munono wa mu mulu
Mulemusangamo;
Alemoneka ku bantu,
Ali mu mukondo."

5. Efyo wa sosa wa ntanshi,
Elyo lyamoneka,
Ibumba lya bangeli,
Balumbanye Lesa:

6. "Ululumbi kuli Lesa,
Mutende mu calo,
Fya busuma fyatendeka
Nomba no kupwa yo."

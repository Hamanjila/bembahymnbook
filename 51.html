51. YESU E KAAFWA WESU

"What a Friend we have in Jesus"


1. Yesu e kaafwa wesu, 
Ulubembu asenda,
Cisuma tutwale fyonse
Kwa Lesa mu kupepa.
Nanga ifwe twayanshiwa,
Twikatisho kulombo;
Nangu tulecula-cula,
Tupepe mu mitima

2. Bushe tuli no kweshiwa?
Bushe, tulecula pe?
Ifyo fine twinenuka,
Kuli Yesu tulombe.
Kaafwa umbi takuli
Wa mpofu no wa luse,
Yesu atwishiba bonse.
E calenga tupepe,

3.  Nga twanaka no kufinwa,
No kumfwisho buali,
Yesu e katula wesu,
Mu mapepo tumwebe.
Ilyo fibusa fyatusaha,
Yesu takatushe yo.
Mu maboko atubaka,
Tukasango butusho.

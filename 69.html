69. KRISTU NASHUKUKE LELO

“Jesus Christ Is risen today”


1. Kristu nashukuke lelo,
Acindikwe Shikulu!
E kasuba ka mushilo:
Acindikwe Shikulu!
Santulweni ulwimbo,
Acindikwe Shikulu!
Mwe ba panshi na ba m’muli,
Acindikwe Shikulu!

2. Nomba tumwimbile nyimbo,
Kristu Mfumu ya mu mulu;
Aculile pa bubi,
Alubula ifwe babi.

3. Imfwa yakwa ya bukali
Yafutila bonse Kale;
E wa mweo, wa maka
Aletula no kubaka.

4. Tucindeke Lesa Mkulu
Lumbeni mwe ba mu mulu;
Ba cikuku, ba luse,
Tata, Mwana, no Mupashi.

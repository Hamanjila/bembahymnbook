234. KULI LESA TULETOTA  

"Nearer my God to thee"

1. Kamupalamine,
Lesa wandi,
Ngo mwenso wandeta
Unko muli;
'Lwimbo Iwandi lube:
Kanupalamine,
Lesa wandi.

2. Nga ndi mu Bulendo,
Naililwa,
'Mfifi yafitisha,
Nalalafye.
Mu ciloto mpepa:
Kamupalamine,
Lesa wandi. 

3. llyo nasamina
Mutwe pe bwe,
No busanshi bwandi
Ni pa nshi fye;
Elyo mpepa, ntila:
Kamupal amine,
Lesa wandi.

4. 'Lintu nashibuka,
Natotela,
E lyo natole bwe,
Ncite Betel.
Mu bulanda ntila:
Kamupalamine,
Lesa wandi.
 
5. 'Lintu nkashe calo,
Nkapupuka,
Nugame ku mulu
Uko muli.
No lu Iwimbo lube:
Kamupalamine.
Lesa wandi.

32. AKAMUSHI BETLEHEME

"O little town of Bethlehem"

 1. Akamushi Betlehemu
Akali tondolo,
Abana bonse ba bantu
Basendamo tulo.
Nangwi mfifi ya bushiku
yali pano panshi,
 Uyo waiso lubuto
Abo Mupusushi.

2. Kuli Mariya afyalilwe
Yesu Kristu, 'Mfumu;
Na bangeli balonene
Lumbula ku mulu.
Mwe ntanda shonse sha mu mulu
Shimikileniko,
'Lulumbi lube kwa Lesa,
'Mutende mu calo.

3. Kanshi ubupe icalo
Capelwa na Lesa;
Nabo bwaisa tondolo
'Kwabulo kukafya.
Ifyo fine na ku bantu
Lesa apele pe;
Ubwa cine ne cikuku
Muli 'fwe fikale.

4. We Mwana wa mushilo, we 
Wa mu Betlehemu,
Wise wikale muli 'fwe,
Ufumyo lubembu.
Twaumfwo lwimbo ku mulu
Lwe landwe lisuma;
mwise kuli 'fwe mwe Mfumu
Mwikale  Katula

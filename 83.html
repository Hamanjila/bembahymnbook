83. MWE MUPU BULESA

"Breathe on me breath of God"


1. Mwe Mupu bulesa,
Iseni mupite,
Ntemwe pe fyo mwatemwa;
Munjimye, nkacite.

2.  Mwe Mupu bulesa, 
Puteni pali ne,
Nkacule ne mwe, washipa,
Washila, wa cine.

3. Mwe Mupu bulesa,
Mwise no kwingila;
Imbe no mweo wacisha,
'Mulilo washila.

4.  Mwe Mupu bulesa,
Nga mwaputa ine,
Nkaba no mweo ushipwa,
kwa Lesa wa cine.

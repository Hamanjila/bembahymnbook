"196. YESU MFUMU ANTEMWA
"Jesus loves me"

1. Yesu Mfumu antenwa,
Ifi fine njishiba,
Bana bonse e bakwe
Banaka, wenya kosa

Yesu antemwa,
Cine antemwa,
Yesu antemwa,
'Fyebo fyanjebe fi

2. Yesu Mfumu antenwa,
'Mulu anjiwilile;
Alansambile fibi,
No kundeko kwingila.

3. Yesu Mfumu antenwa,
Mupepi muno nshila,
Nana nga namutemwa,
Akasenda ku mulu.

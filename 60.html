60. EE BULANDA

1. Ee bulanda!
Ifilamba fyandi
Fipone pa makasa ya Mfumu

2. Ee bulanda!
Mfumu munjelele
Ne wa mpulumushi napapata.

3. Ee bulanda!
Ifyo mwanculile!
Bubi bwandi e bwamucushishe.

4. Ee bulanda!
Filamba fyafula
Umwenso wanjikata, ntuleni.

5. Ee bulanda!
Ishiwi ndeufwa
“Tata mubelele tabeshibe.”

6. Ee Kutemwa,
Kwa kwa Lesa wesu,
Kwasokoloka mu lupanda lu!

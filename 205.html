205. TATA TULEMUOTELA
"Father, we thank Thee for the night"

1.  Tata tulemutotela,
Pa budhiku na kasuba,
Butusho, filyo no luse,
Ne fyatupe nseko fyonse.

2. Mutwafwe tucite bwino,
No kuba ba luse 'lelo,
Mu milimo no kwangala,
Tukule mu bwina Lesa.

115. MWE BA SHIFWE, KALENGA

"O Father, all creating"

1. Mwe ba Shifwe, Kalenga,
Mano, luse ,maka,
Mu Edene mwaufya,
Mwi bala mwabaka;
Ku bwinga buno mwise,
Mulete ;fya bupe,-
Babe fye no mutende
No kutemwana pe.

2. Mwe Yesu mwafumbile
Ku bwinga ku kana,
E po mube, mupale,
Abaleupana;
Ku musamwe bamwene
Musanshe no wenu,
Pa kusonda beshibe
 E ca bupe cenu.

3.  Mwe Mupashi wa cine,
Wa luse, washila,
Mwise mubaponene,
Mulekukutila;
Mufunde no kuwamya,
Baleba bafuka;
Muteke no kubensha,
Baleba bashuka.

4. Abakula apafye, Tata,
Bashaba na Imwe;
Yesu tapala ng'anda,
Tamwaba aba musamwe;
Ababo mo muli mwe,
Takuli cingona;
Uluse no mutende
E fyo bakamona.

201. YESU UWALI MU MULU
"Jesus who lives above the sky"

1. Yesu uwali mu mulu
Aishile pano muntu,
Mu lupapulo tumone
Ifyo wene aliwama.

2. Aendawike, ni Mfumu,
Apelya menso ku mpofu;
Aposeshe abalwele
Ababelele uluse.

3. Elyo abelele cine
Ca kwa Lesa, bacicite;
E wa cikuku, afuka,
Atemwo kumfwa ku Mwana.

4. Abantu bamufyengele,
Aimikwe, atanikwe,
Yalya mabako yawama
Ku muti bapampamine.

5. Awe! E co afumine
Ku mulu ni ku kwisa fwa;
Amalembo yaletweba:
Aleelela babipa. 

6. Aishibe balibipa,
Ne fibi filabacusha,
Elyo asosa mu luse,
Nintemwa kuti mbafwile.

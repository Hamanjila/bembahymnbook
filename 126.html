126. MWE KATULA MWINCILILA

"Pass me not, O gentle Saviour"

1. Mwe katula mwincilila,
Ndemupapata,
Ilyo muleita bambi,
Mwilancilila.

Yesu, Yesu,
Mumfwe ndekuta,
Ilyo muleita bambi,
mwilancilila,

2. Kampalame kuli Imwe,
Mpele uluse,
Kamfukame no bufuke,
Nsangyo mutende.

3.  Mubusuma bwenu bweka
Ncetekela pe,
Ku poshiwa mu mutima
E cabupa fye.

4. Nimwe mweo ne cimwemwe,
Nimwe cikuku;
Umbi panshi nshimumwene,
nangu mu mulu.

174. MWE MFUMU NYENDE NA IMWE
“O Master, let me walk with Thee”

1. Mwe Mfumu, nyende na imwe
Mu nshila sha milimo pe;
Munjebe bwino mungafwe,
Mu mulilo no bulanda.

2. Mungafwe ncincishe bantu
Ne shiwi lya luse lwenu;
Ndetungulula baluba,
Ku nshilla yenu yawama.

3. Mumpelyo mute’ na imwe
Nyende mu bucibusa pe
Mu milimo ncetekela
Mwi subilo nkacimfya.

4. Lubuto lwenu lube pe
Mu nshila yandi panonse;
‘Mutende wenu mumpele,
Mwe Mfumu njikale ne mwe.

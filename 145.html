145. BWELA WE WALUBA, WE WANAKA

"Turn thee, O lost one"

1.  Bwela we waluba, we wanaka
Mona kacema alepapata;
Alefwaya ukukupususha,
Mupokelele, wilakokola.

Alepapata, aleita pe,
Umfwa Kacema alekwita,
Alepapata, aleita pe,
Alesosa, "Isa kuli 'ine!"

2. Alekupembela we ulefwa,
We walolele kutali kwine
Aliposele umweo wakwe
Kuti umone umweo iwe.

3. Umfwa imbila lya kwa Katula,
Asendele mpulumushi
Akupelyo umweo wa pe na pe.

4. Isa mu musango wa cine,
Yesu e mwinshi wa kwingililapo
Kacema alekwitano luse;
Wingilemo tekuti ulube.

140. KUFWAYA ABALUBA KWEKA

"Seeking the lost"

1. Kufwaya abaluba kweka,
No kupapata luse luse,
Yesu ati "Seni kuli ine"
Efyo ne lelo akebeta.

Akauyeni . . . mu tumishi tonse . . .
'Kubaleta . . . abaluba . . .
'Kubaleta . . . ku Mwine kacema .
Yesu Mwana . . . wa kubafwila.

2 Tubafishe kuli Yesu umwine,
Abanaka na ba cula,
No kubalanga ipusukilo,
Beshbe mwa kumweno mweo 

3 Tufwaya kube nkombe sha luse.
'Kukonka Kristu 'nshiku shonse
Twafwe abawa nabwa banaka,
Baluba tubalange Yesu.

4 Tucetekele nshita ikesa,
Yeswa 'kabwela mu lulumbi,
Natwipeleshe ku milimo yu,
Banenswa bengi bapusuke.

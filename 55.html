55. KULI KAPILI ‘KUTALI

“There is a green hill far away

1. Kuli kapili ‘kutali
Ku musumba ku nse,
Uko Kristu atanikwe,
Atule few bonse.

2. Te’ti twishibe ‘fikali
Ifyo aumfwili;
Twasubila ni pali ‘few
We ne aculile.

3. Atufwilile few babi,
Tube abawama;
Tufike ukwaikala
Basambwa, basaama.

4. Takwali umbi musuma
Wa kutufutila;
Aisulako ku mulu,
Alanga nshila.

5. Atutemwesha few bonse
Tuletetekela;
Tumutemweshe na ifwe,
No kumubombela.

27. SHISENI, MWE IMMANUEL

"O come, O come Immanuel"

 1. Shiseni, mwe Immanuel,
Mwise lubula Israel,
Ku bunkole alelosha
Asuke 'se Mwana Lesa.

Anga! Anga! Immanuel,
Aleisa,we Israel.

2. 'Seni, Nkonto ya kwa Yesa,
Iseni, benu mutashe;
Batuleni ku Gehena,
Bacimfye imfwa ne Ciwa.

3. Shiseni, mwe Kasuba,
Tabutusheni mitima;
Ikumbi 'li litamfiwe,
No bushiku bwa mfwa bupwe.

4. Shiseni, mwe Lufungulo,
Mutwiswileko ku mulu;
Muwamishe mu nshila ine,
Ya ku bucushi mushile.

5. Shiseni, mwe Mfumu Nkulu,
Mwe bapele 'bantu benu
'Mafunde yene pa Sinai,
Mu kutinya no mucinshi.

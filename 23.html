23. LESA EKACEMA WANDI  

"The Lord is my shepherd"

1. Lesa ekacema wandi,
Nshakabile pe,
Ku mulemfwe alentwala,
Ku menshi ya bumi.

2. Umutima ambwesesha,
Mu Iwendo angafwa,
Mu nshila sha bololoke,
Pe shina lyakwe fye. 

3. 'Lintu nkenda mu mfwa ine
Nshakatine kantu,
Pantu Imwe mulensuiga.
No kunsansamusha. 

4 'Cintamba mwanungikila.
Mu cinso ca babi,
Umutwe wandi rnwansuba,
'Nkombo shaisula. 


5 Busuma bweka no luse,
Fyakulankonka pe,
Na mu ng'anda ya kwa Lesa,
Nkekalililamo.

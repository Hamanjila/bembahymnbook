200.  NDESEKELA TATA WA MU MULU

"I am so glad that our father in heaven"

1. Ndesekela Tata wa mu mulu
Anjebele fya kutemwa kwakwe:
Mu kalata ndemone fisuma,
Icacisha, Yesu alintemwa.

Ndesekelela, alintemwa,
Yesu, Yesu, alintemwa,
Ndesekelela, alintemwa,
Yesu alintemwa.
2. Nga namulaba no kwendauka  
Alentemwa pe nangula nduba;  
Ndebwelela bwangu kwa Katula.
Pa kwibukishe fyo alintemwa.

3. Lwimbo Iwandi ilyo nkalaimba,
Nga namone Mfumu ikankala
Mu kucindama kwakwe kwakula,
Ndesunguka. Yesu alintemwa.

4. Ndemutemwisha Yesu ne mwane;
Antemenwe, e co andumbwile,
Amfutilo bubi no mulopa;
'Cine cine, Yesu alintemwa.

5. Nga bambusha "Waishiba shani"
Nati, "Pantu Yasu amfwilile"
Mupashi wakwe amhimikila
Anange fyo Yasu alintamwa.
 
6. Ndi no mutende pakwishibisha;
Ndecetekela, nasansamuka;
Nombo mulwani afulumuka,
'Co namweba, "Yesu alintamwa.

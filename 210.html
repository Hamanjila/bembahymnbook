210. MWE SHIFWE WA MU MULU
IPEPO LWA KWA SHIKULWIFWE.


Mwe shifwe wa mu mulu,
Ishina lwenu licindikwe,
Ubufumu Bwenu bwuse;
Ukufwaya kwenu kucitwe panonse nga mu mulu
Mutupele ifyakulya fyesu ifya lelo.
Mutulekele imisha yesu, ifyp na ifwe tulekela abatulile misha.
(Mutwelele milandu yesu, ngefyo na ifwe twelala abatubale imilandu).
Mwitutwala mukweshowa, lelo mututule ku bubi.
Pantu ubufumu, na maka, no kucindama fyenu'
umuyayaya. Amen,

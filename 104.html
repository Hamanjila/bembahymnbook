104. BULENI UBWIKASHI

 "Take my life and let it be"


1. Buleni ubwikashi,
Mupelwe no bu bwine;
Utushita ne nshiku,
'Kuti mulumbanyemo.

2. Buleni amaboko, 
Kamubombele nayo;
'Makaso kuti yabe
ya lubilo kuli 'mwe.

3. Buleni shiwi lyandi,
'Kuti mwimbile imwe,
'Milomo kuti ibe,
Na mashiwi kuli 'mwe. 

4.  Buleni fyuma fyandi
Teti mukaninine;
Bulenya maka mano,
Kamubombele nayo.

5.  Buleni mifwayile,
Nomba taili yandi,
'Mutima ube wenu,
Kuti mwikalemo pe.

6.  Bulenyu luse ulu,
Lintu mwampa mwe Mfumu;
Mumbule ime wine
'Kuti mbe wenu wa pe.

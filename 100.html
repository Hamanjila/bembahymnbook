100. MUTUFUMYE NO BUPALO

"Lord, dismiss us with thy blessing"


1. Mutufumye no bupalo
Mu mitima yese pe;
Tube no kutemwa kweni,
Twange mu luse lwenu;
Mutukoshe, mutukoshe,
Pwendela panonse.

2. No mucinshi twatotela 
Pa mashiwi yasuma;
Yabe fyakulya fya mweo,
Yekale mu mitima;
'Cinso cenu, 'cinso cenu
Cibe ne fwe nshiku pe.

3.  Elyo lintu twapalama
No kufuma mu calo,
Ni ku mulu tukasendwa,
Tunakile ne nseko;
Tukekala, tukekala,
Na Yesu ishiku pe.

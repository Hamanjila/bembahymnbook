58. ‘CINE YESU ANCULILE
“Alas! And did my saviour”

1. ‘Cine Yesu anculile,
‘Mfumu yamfwilile;
‘Mfumu wakwe walashilwa
Ine muntu mubi.
2. Ni pa bupulumushi bwandi
Atetele Kale;
Ku lupanda Kwamoneke
Ubwingi bwa luse.
3. ‘Lintu Kristu e Katula
Afwililya bantu,
Kasuba takamoneke,
Takabalike yo.
4. ‘Filamba fyandi fyacepa
Ubupe ku Mfumu;
Kuli imwe ndeipela,
Eco ndecita fye.

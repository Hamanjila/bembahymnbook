103. KATULA AISA KANA

"A little child the Saviour came"  


1. Katula aisa kana,
Lesa wamaka lishina;
Baisatota bangeli
Kwati fye mwana alele.

2.  Uwatendeka ku bunya
Ukulangd Lesa sana,
Aleti mu mulu mwe,
'Abana bese kuli ne!

3.  Natuleta aba bana,
Kuli 'mwe mu ng'anda yenu
Ku Mupashi mubatishe,
Ku luse lwenu mubishe.

4.  Nkombe shenu shibabake,
Bende mutende mu nshila,
Mubake kasunga wabo,
Mubalembe na mashina.

5.  Mwe bomfya bwino ku bana
Malumbo yenu yayana,
Lekeni nu tu tutashe
Shifwe, Mwana, no Mupashi.

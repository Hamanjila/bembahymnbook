225. YEMWE BETLEHEMU

1. Yemwe Betlehemu
E mushi uwapalwa,
 Yemwe Betlehemu
E mushi uwapalwa.
 
2. Mary anakile
Pa kufika ku mushe
Balangilwe mulya,
Mwi tanga lya ng'ombe.

3. Bushiku bwine ube
Lutanda lwamoneka
Lwaibele sana
Ku ntanda sha mulu.

4. Bakakumba eba-
Balilyo kumfwa mbila,
Mungeli aikile
Abebele cine.
 
5. Mwe Mfumu twatotela
Pa bushiku busuma,
Pa bafyashi besu,
Pa bumu busuma.

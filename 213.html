213. AMASHIWI YE SHUKO (MATEO 5: 3-10)

1. balishuka ababusu mu mitima; pantu ubufumu bwa mu mulu bwabo.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
2. balishuka abalosha; pantu bene bakasansamushiwa.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
3. Balishuka abafuka; pantu bakakwate calo.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
4. balishuka abali ne nsala ne cilaka ca bulungami; pantu bene bakekuta.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
5. balishuka aba luse; pantu bakabelelwo luse.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
6. balishuka abasanguluke mitima; pantu baka mona Lesa.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
7. balishuka abacito mutende; pantu baketwa abana ba kwa Lesa.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
8. balishuka abalamatilwa pa mulandu wa bulungami; pantu ubufuma bwa mu mulu bwabo.
R Tushuke fi, Mfumu, twapapata.
9. Muli abashuka ilyo bamupontela, no kumulamata, no kumwambo bufi icintu conse ca bubifi pa mulandu wa ine; sekeleleni no kwanga, pantu icilambu cenu cikalamba mu mulu; pantu e fyo balamete bakasesema ba ntanshi.
R Lembeni fye shuko, mu mitima, Mfumu.

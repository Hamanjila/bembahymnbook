68. KATULA WANDI WATUNGULULWA

“Mporoski warongozgeka”


1. Katula wandi watungululwa
Kwati ni mpanga, bubi bwandi.

2. Alipepele mu Getsemane
Bushiku bonse, bubi bwandi.

3. Katula wandi alitanikwe
Asumya m’lopa, bubi bwandi.

4. Alimfwilile pa capindama
Alishikilwe, bubi bwandi.

5. Alibushuwe mulya mu nshinshi
Acimfya imfwa, bubi bwandi.

5. Alimine kuya ku mulu
Kumpapatila, bubi bwandi.

6. Katula umo mu calo conse
Wakumpususha, bubi bwandi.

168. MWE CENGE CA LUSE, NTUNGULUSHI
" Lead, kindly Light, amid the encircling gloom"

1. Mwe cenge ca luse, Ntungulushi,
Nyensheni mwe;
Bwaila ye, kwalepa ku mushi!
Nyensheni mwe. 
Nshileti mone ifya kutali;
Ndeyenda naikato to muli

2. Kale nshali ifyo, nshali walomba,
"Nyensheni mwe,"
Nali wasala fye nshila nomba
Nyensheni mwe;
Nali wapumba, nati njikale,
Mwe Lesa, Ye! labeni sha kale.

3. Mwe bafwa kuli nomba, nyensheni;
Ifyo fine.
Mwafwe mu mpili, m'nika. cesheni
Ubwa cine:
Elyo kwaula nkomfwa kwashila,
Kwa batemwikwa batangilila.

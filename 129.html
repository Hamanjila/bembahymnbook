129.  MWISE KWA YESU, MWIKOKOLA

"Come to the saviour, make no delay"

1. Mwise kwa Yesu, mwikokola,
Ku mbila kwamoneke nshila;
Ali mupepi, aleita,
Aleti, "Iseni."

Tukasamwa pa kumonana,
Nga twasambwa twaba bashila;
Tukekala ne mwe, Ka tula,
Ku mwesu nshiku pe.

2. Mumunakile uwatile,
"Abana bese kuli Ine"
Aleni Yesu tumutemwe.
Mwikokola, seni.

3 Tontonkanyeni e po ali,
Ishiwi lyakwe shiumfweni,
Aleti, "Imwe bana bandi,
Mwikokola. 'seni"

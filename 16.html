16. . MAKANA NA MAKANA

"Ten thousand times "

 1. . Makana na makana
Ya bafwikwa tutu,
Ifita fya balubulwa 
Fyanina ku mulu,
Fyapwa! fyonse nafipwa,
Ukulwa ne fibi;
Beswileni ifipata,
Aba kabengile.

Aleluya! Aleluya!
kuuwatufwilile;
Aleluya! Aleluya!
Nao alimine.

2. 'Nyimbo sha Aleluya
Sha calo no mulu!
'Kulisha kwa masese kwa
Twebelyo bwanshisho.
We bushiku busuma,
Bwa fyabumbwa fyonse;
We nseko, ulefumyapo
Ubulanda bonse.

3. E, tukaceleshanya
Mu mulu mwi shilya;
Pa kukumanye fubusa
No kushilekana.
Amenso yakawama,
'Yalile filamba,
Tabalimo bakashiwa,
Neyo mukamfwilwa.

4. Mwe Mfumu, bupalame
'Bupususho bwenu;
Ba ku mulu mubalembe,
Mwe ka mube Mfumu.
mwe bafwaikwa, mwise,
'Basha bafuluka;
'Bulangililo mubape,
Mwe Mfumu, katula.

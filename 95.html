95. MWI SHINA LYA KWA YESU

"Father, again in Jesus, Name we meet"


1. Mwi shina lya kwa Yesu, Mwe Tata,
Natulongana, tulepapata;
Mube no luse, tuli bapumbwa,
Namumfwe nyimbo sha kumulumba.

2.  Mwe batusakamana nshike pe,
Ku luse lwenu mupe fya bupe;
Mwe batucita pe 'fye bukata,
Ku minwe yenu muletwikata.

3.  Na ifwe twaba bafye, twaluba,
Ububi bwine bwaisabuba
Tuli fye no bulanda, mwe Tata,

4. Mwi shina lya kwa Yesu 'mwapaka,
Mu luse lwenu lwine lwa ntaka,
Mu m'lopo waitilwe pali fwe,
Twiswileniko nomba mwe Shifwe.

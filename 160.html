160. MWE MFUMU M NSOSHE, NSOSE
"Lord speak to me that speak"

1. Mwe Mfumu munsoshe, nsose
Misango yenu ya mweo,
Ifyo mufwaya, kamfwaye
Abana benwa naluba.

2. Mutungulule 'ne wine,
Ntungulule abaluba,
Mundisho kuti 'ne ndishe
Abana benwa na nsala.

3. Munkoshe mu kwiminina
Pa libwe lya maka yenu,
Ntambikyo kuboko kwandi,
Kuli abo abanwena.

4. Mumfundisho kuti mfunde
Fintu fyenu ifya cine,
'Mashiwi yandi yengile
Mu ba mitima yakosa.

5. Mumpelyo butushoo bwenu,
'Kuti nsose no kutemwa;
Amashiwi ayalinga
Ku bafilwa, 'ba bulanda.

6. Mumpale mwe Mfumu yandi,
Kutyu mutima wisule,
Matontonkanyo, 'mashiwi
Yalango kutemwa kwine.

7. Munengyo Kumubombela
Uko konse mwansontela,
Nsuke mumoneku mpumi,
Yalango kutemwa kwine.

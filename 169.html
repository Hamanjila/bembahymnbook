169. MWISE NJAFWA, LESA M'KULU

"Guide me, O Thou great Jehovah"

1. Mwise njafwa, Lesa m'kulu,
Ukwenda muno calo;
Nanaka, muli na maka,
M'njikate ku kuboko;
Cilyo cine ca mulu,
Mulendisha mu calo.

2. Ku ntulo mone amenshi
'Yabeka, mpembula;
No m'lilo wenu ne kumbi
Fibe fintungulula;
Mwe bakosa, mwe Katula,
Ncingeni pe ku maka.

3. Pa kufika ku m'longa wa mfwa,
Munjikate, munyenshe;
Imwe mwacimfishe imfwa,
Munjabushe, mumfishe:
Nkamulumba, mwe Kacimfya,
Amalumbo aya pe.

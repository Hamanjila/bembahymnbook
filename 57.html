57. WA KUCULA ISHINA
“Man of sorrows!”


1. Wa kucula ishina
Lya Mwana wa kwa Lesa,
Alubwilya babipa,
Aleluya, Mupusushi!

2. ‘Bantu bamusoweke,
Amfwilile ne mubi,
‘Mulopa wansumine,
Aleluya, Mupusushi!

3. Twabipilwa, twabipa
Wene e wa kaele,
Bushe apwo kutula?
Aleluya, Mupusushi!

4. Aimikwe ku kufwa,
Akutile “Nacipwa,”
Mu mulu nomba ‘fika
Aleluya, Mupusushi!

5. Ikese Mfumu Yesu,
‘Kututwala ku mwesu,
Tukembila mu mulu,
Aleluya, Mupusushi!
